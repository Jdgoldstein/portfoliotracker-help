Setup steps — do these once
1
Install it
Windows: run the downloaded .exe installer.

Mac: open the downloaded .dmg and drag Portfolio Tracker into your Applications folder.
2
Approve it on first launch Once only
Because the app isn't from a registered store publisher yet, your OS asks you to approve it the first time — once per machine.

Windows: if you see "Windows protected your PC," click More info → Run anyway.

Mac: double-click the app; when it says it can't be verified, click Done. Then open System Settings → Privacy & Security, scroll down to Security, click Open Anyway next to the app's name, and confirm with your password. (On macOS Sequoia and later, the old right-click → Open no longer works — you must use System Settings.)
Mac note: after the one-time approval it opens normally every time. A future signed & notarized Mac build will remove this step entirely.
3
Start your trial & register
Launch the app and click ▶ Start Free Trial — 7 days of full use, with your data saved automatically.

To keep using it after the trial, open the in-app registration (name & email). Once your request is approved, the app activates itself automatically — no codes to copy by hand.
