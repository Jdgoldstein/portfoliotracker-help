📱 Phone Alerts — Pushover
Pushover delivers instant alerts to your phone whenever a GEM hits a buy trigger, stop loss, take-profit target, or moves beyond your alert threshold — works from any device, no browser required.
Get keys at pushover.net (~$5 one-time app purchase). Enter both keys below to activate.
